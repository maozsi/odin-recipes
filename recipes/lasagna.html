Lasagna

Mama's Best Lasagna

The best lasagna is here! Layered with a delicious tomato sauce, creamy parmesan white sauce and topped with fresh mozzarella; you will be craving this lasagna every weekend.

Ingredients

Meat sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 carrot finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 24 ounces ground beef mince | 700 grams
  • 10 ounces ground pork mince | 300 grams
  • 24 ounces passata | 700 grams
  • 14 ounces crushed tomatoes | 410 grams
  • 3 tomato paste heaped tablespoons
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes or vegetables, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Half teaspoon sugar
  • Half teaspoon salt season to the taste
  • Quarter teaspoon black pepper to the taste

White sauce | Béchamel

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Quarter cup flour all purpose or plain
  • 3 and a half cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan fresh

Lasagna

  • 13 ounces fresh lasagna sheets | 375 grams
  • 17 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese shredded | 500 grams
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley finely chopped

Instructions

Meat sauce

  1. Heat oil over medium heat, then add the onion and carrots. Cook for 8-10 minutes. Add in the garlic and sauté for one minute.
  2. Add beef and pork, and cook while breaking it up with the end of the spoon, until browned.
  3. Pour in passata, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, crushed bouillon, and dried herbs. Mix well to combine and bring to a gentle simmer. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and cook for about 20-30 minutes, occasionally mixingg, until the sauce has thickened slightly and meat is tender.
  5. Adjust for salt, pepper, and dried herbs.

White sauce | Béchamel

  1. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Remove from hot plate, add the flour and whisk for 30 seconds.
  2. Place pot back onto stove, reduce heat down to low and slowly whisk in one cup milk until well combined. Once blended, add the remainig cups of milk (one at a time) . Mix well after each addition until all the milk is used and sauce has no lumps. If the sauce is too thick, add more milk until it has a nice and creamy consistency.
  3. Ingrease heat to medium and continue cooking sauce while stirring occasionaly until it thickens (around 6 to 7 minutes), and coats the back of the wooden spoon.
  4. Add in the parmesan cheese and remove from heat. Season with salt and pepper and mix until the cheese is melted through.

Assemble

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F | 180 °C
  2. Spoon about one cupe of meat sauce on the base of a 9x13-inch baking dish, cover with lasagna sheets. Trim sheets to lift over the meat if needed. Layer with two cups of meat sauce, or enough to cover pasta, one cup of white sauce and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers but remember to leave the remainder of the cheese for the top.
  3. Pour the remainig meat sauce and white sauce over the last layer of lasagna sheets and top with the remainig mozzarella cheese. Bake for 25 minutes.
  4. Garnish with parsley and let stand for about ten minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy!
